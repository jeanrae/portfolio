About me

I’m a UX/UI designer passionate about connecting with and learning from people. I strive to build products that bridge the gap between people and their goals in an intuitive and joyful way. With a background in journalism and education, communication is at the centre of my process.

Travelling is one of my greatest joys and I’ve been to 40 countries and lived in Canada, Denmark, Vietnam and Germany. I believe travelling has improved my communication and observation skills and helped me be a more empathetic and adaptable person. I’m at my happiest when discovering new cities, hiking trails, foods or design inspiration.
